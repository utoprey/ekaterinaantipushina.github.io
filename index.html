About Me

Currently pursuing my PhD at Skoltech's Applied AI Center, where I explore the fascinating intersection of technology and human experience. My research focuses on making AI more intuitive and accessible, particularly in medical applications.

fun fact: I believe the best AI solutions are the ones that feel most human!

My journey began with biomedical engineering at Moscow Aviation Institute, where I developed a deep appreciation for how technology can enhance human wellbeing. This foundation led me to specialize in Data Science at Skoltech, focusing on interpretable machine learning for multimodal neuroimaging data.

When I'm not diving deep into neural networks or analyzing brain connectivity patterns, you might find me sketching out ideas for more intuitive user interfaces or exploring Moscow's cozy cafés for inspiration.

Creating quantization techniques that turn brain signals into meaningful digital tokens. It's like teaching computers to understand the language of neurons in a way that preserves all the important information.

decoding minds! 🧠💫

pyOpenNFT
2024
Reimagined real-time fMRI neurofeedback with a fully Python architecture. No more clunky MATLAB dependencies! Built user-friendly APIs that make neurofeedback accessible to researchers everywhere.
Python FastAPI NumPy OpenCV Lab Streaming Layer

real-time brain magic! ⚡

Rest2Task
2023
Developed a generative framework that creates task-based fMRI data from resting scans. Achieved impressive results (MSE of 0.05 ± 0.01) while maintaining the biological authenticity of brain patterns.
VAE Conditional GAN Neural Optimal Transport Docker

dreaming up brain states! 🌙

2023
Used geometric features and topological data analysis to predict schizophrenia. It's like finding hidden patterns in the brain's geometry that can help with early diagnosis and better understanding.
