About Me

Currently pursuing my PhD at Skoltech's Applied AI Center & Vladimar Zelman Neurocenter. My research focuses on developing AI frameworks for medical applications, particularly in neuroimaging and neurofeedback systems. I specialize in multimodal data fusion approaches, foundation model development with neural quantization techniques, and generative AI methods (VAEs, GANs, Neural Optimal Transport) for analyzing brain signals like EEG and fMRI.

fun fact: outside the lab, you'll find me in Moscow's cozy cafés seeking inspiration.

My Research Interests

Generative AI

  • Foundation models with temporal encoding
  • Neural quantization for continuous-to-discrete conversion
  • Cross-modal learning from 1D to 4D neural signals
  • Self-supervised learning for multichannel data

Medical AI & Neuroimaging

  • EEG, fMRI analysis
  • EEG-to-fMRI problem solutions using deep learning
  • Data analysis for schizophrenia prediction
  • Pharmacological biomarker discovery in brain pathologies
